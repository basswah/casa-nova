# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Shop owners and cashiers in Syria. Bilingual Arabic/English. They operate in a fast-paced retail environment handling walk-in customers, managing inventory, and processing sales. The primary interaction is through a desktop browser at the point of sale.

## Product Purpose

Casa Nova POS is a modern point-of-sale and inventory management system for Syrian retail businesses. It handles dual-currency pricing (USD/SYP), tracks inventory in real time, manages purchase orders and suppliers, and provides sales reporting. The goal is to replace paper-based or legacy systems with a clean, fast digital tool.

## Brand Personality

Modern, Clean, Fast. Professional but not cold. The brand communicates reliability and efficiency through restraint. Every interaction should feel immediate: no loading spinners where instant updates are possible, no unnecessary clicks for common tasks.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

1. **Speed is the feature.** The POS workflow must feel instantaneous. Optimize for the cashier, not the developer. The checkout flow should be the fastest path in the app.
2. **Clarity over density.** Retail counters are busy environments. Screens must be scannable at a glance. Use generous whitespace, clear visual hierarchy, and avoid information overload.
3. **Bilingual by default.** Arabic and English share the same visual weight. Layouts must flex between LTR and RTL without breaking. Never design for one language then translate.
4. **Progressive complexity.** Common tasks (sell, check stock) are one click away. Advanced features (reports, settings, returns) are available but never clutter the primary workflow.
5. **Dark mode is the default.** Cashiers work under varied lighting. The dark theme reduces eye strain and puts focus on content. Light mode exists as an alternative, not the primary presentation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific WCAG requirements. The dark/light theme toggle accommodates different lighting conditions. Bilingual RTL/LTR support is built in.
